Scoring guide (0-5)
  • Intelligibility（可懂度）: Is the speech clear and easy to understand? (0 = cannot understand, 5 = very clear)
  • Naturalness（自然度）: Does the speech sound natural and human-like? (0 = very unnatural, 5 = very natural)
  • Emotional expressiveness（情感表达丰富度）: How accurately and richly is the target emotion expressed? (0 = wrong/no emotion, 5 = very accurate and expressive)
  • Overall quality（音频质量）: Overall audio quality including noise, distortion, and smoothness. (0 = very poor, 5 = excellent)
